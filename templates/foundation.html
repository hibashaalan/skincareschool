Cleanser

The first and most essential step in skincare. It removes dirt, sweat, excess oil, makeup, sunscreen, pollution, and bacteria.

Step 1: Oil-based cleanser — Breaks down makeup, sunscreen, and sebum
Step 2: Water-based cleanser — Removes sweat and water-based impurities

Note: Use both (double cleanse) for a deep clean, or just a water-based cleanser if you don’t wear makeup or heavy sunscreen.

Toner

Balances skin after cleansing.
It removes leftover residue, restores pH, and preps skin to absorb serums/moisturizers.

Dry Skin
Goal: Deep hydration
Ingredients: Hyaluronic acid, aloe vera, panthenol

Oily Skin
Goal: Control oil, unclog pores
Ingredients: Witch hazel, niacinamide, salicylic acid

Normal Skin
Goal: Maintain hydration
Ingredients: Rose water, hyaluronic acid, glycerin

Combination Skin
Goal: Balance dry and oily areas
Ingredients: Green tea, niacinamide, rose water

Serums

Delivers concentrated ingredients to target specific skin concerns.
Apply after toner, before moisturizer.

Hydration:
Hyaluronic acid

Brightening:
Vitamin C

Acne:
Salicylic acid

Pores:
Niacinamide

Anti-aging:
Retinol

Tip: Use 2–3 drops, gently press into skin.

Eye Creams

Targets the delicate under-eye area.
Use after serum, before moisturizer.

Dark circles:
Caffeine

Brightening:
Vitamin C

Puffiness:
Green tea extract, peptides

Fine lines:
Retinol (low %, gentle), peptides

Tip: Use your ring finger to tap the product on—never rub.

Moisturizers

Seals in hydration and strengthens the skin barrier.
Apply after serum/eye cream, before SPF (AM).

Dry Skin:
Thick creams with ceramides, shea butter

Oily Skin:
Lightweight gel with glycerin, aloe

Combination Skin:
Balancing lotions with niacinamide

Normal Skin:
Hydrating creams with squalane, hyaluronic acid

Tip: Don’t skip—even oily skin needs moisture!

Sunscreen

Protects your skin from UV damage.
It prevents sunburn, premature aging, dark spots, and skin cancer.
Use every morning, even on cloudy days or indoors.

Mineral (physical):
Zinc oxide, titanium dioxide — best for sensitive skin

Chemical:
Avobenzone, octocrylene — lighter feel, blends easily

Pick SPF 30+ and broad-spectrum (protects from UVA & UVB).
Reapply every 2 hours if you’re outside.

Tip: Don’t forget your neck, ears, and hands!
