Learn about different skin types

Normal Skin

Even texture, even tone, small pores, blemish-free

Oily Skin

Shiny appearance, enlarged pores, prone to acne

Dry Skin

Coarse texture, tight pores, visible fine lines

Combination Skin

Large pores, dry in some areas, blemished
Normal Skin
Normal skin is well-balanced, not too oily or dry. It features smooth texture, small pores, and even tone.
Oily Skin
Oily skin produces excess sebum, leading to shine, visible pores, and frequent breakouts. Look for lightweight, non-comedogenic products.
Dry Skin
Dry skin often feels tight, rough, and flaky. Use hydrating ingredients like hyaluronic acid and avoid harsh cleansers.
Combination Skin
Combination skin features both dry and oily areas. The T-zone may be shiny while the cheeks remain dry.
