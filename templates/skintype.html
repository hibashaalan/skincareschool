Normal Skin

Appearance:

  • Even texture
  • Small, barely visible pores
  • No pronounced oiliness or dryness

Care Tips:

  • Gentle, pH-balanced cleanser once or twice daily
  • Light exfoliation 1×/week
  • Daily hydrating toner or essence
Oily Skin

Appearance:

  • Noticeable shine, especially in the T-zone (forehead, nose, chin)
  • Enlarged pores, prone to blackheads
  • Prone to breakouts and acne

Care Tips:

  • Oil-control cleanser with salicylic acid or zinc PCA
  • Gentle exfoliation (BHA) 1–2×/week to clear pores
  • Lightweight, non-comedogenic moisturizer
Dry Skin

Appearance:

  • Tight or rough patches, especially after cleansing
  • Flakiness or redness around t-zone
  • More visible fine lines

Care Tips:

  • Creamy, non-stripping cleanser (no sulfates)
  • Hydrating masks (sheet or overnight) 1–2×/week
  • Seal in moisture with occlusive ingredients
Combination Skin

Appearance:

  • Oily/shiny T-zone (forehead, nose, chin)
  • Dry or normal cheeks
  • Mixed pore sizes (larger in T-zone)

Care Tips:

  • Use a gentle foaming cleanser all over
  • Targeted treatments: BHA in oily zones, hydrating serums on dry areas
  • Layer lightweight and richer products where needed
