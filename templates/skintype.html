Normal Skin

Normal skin is well-balanced, not too oily or dry. It has a smooth texture and even tone. It is less prone to breakouts and irritation.

  • Even texture
  • Even tone
  • Small pores
  • Blemish free
Oily Skin

Oily skin produces excess sebum, leading to shine, visible pores, and frequent breakouts. Look for lightweight, non-comedogenic products.

  • Shiny appearance
  • Enlarged pores
  • Prone to acne and blackheads
Dry Skin

Dry skin lacks moisture and can feel tight or rough. It may appear dull and flaky. Look for rich, hydrating products.

  • Coarse texture
  • Tight pores
  • Visible fine lines
Combination Skin

Combination skin features both dry and oily areas. The T-zone may be shiny while the cheeks remain dry.

  • Large pores
  • Thicker texture
  • Dry in some areas
  • Blemished
